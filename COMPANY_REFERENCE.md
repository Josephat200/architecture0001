## About
PlanKraft International is a multi-disciplinary firm focused on the built environment. Our core specialties include Architectural Design, Project Management, Model Making, Interior Design & Landscaping. We blend creativity with technical precision, delivering bespoke design solutions that are structurally sound and visually compelling.

## Philosophy
We create functional, sustainable, and contextual built environments, combining local craft with cutting-edge technology. We operate to international standards and collaborate with global partners.
