Kenyan Architects Declare
Climate & Biodiversity Emergency

Grasp Design Ltd has taken a leadership role in the built environment sector by initiating the establishment of the Kenyan chapter of Architects Declare, a global movement addressing the climate and biodiversity emergency.

The firm approached five other local architectural practices to join this international initiative, uniting Kenya’s built environment professionals in a collective response to the urgent challenges of climate change and biodiversity loss.

This action demonstrates Grasp Design Ltd’s commitment to socio-environmental impact, global collaboration, and industry leadership—connecting local expertise with a worldwide movement for a more sustainable future.
