WHAT WE DO

At Plankraft International, we provide comprehensive architectural and design services that respond to the evolving needs of the built environment. Our work spans across multiple sectors, combining creativity, technical expertise, and sustainable thinking to deliver solutions that are both functional and visually compelling.

We approach every project holistically—from initial concept development to final execution—ensuring quality, efficiency, and innovation at every stage.

OUR CORE SERVICES
Architectural Design

We specialize in the design of modern, innovative, and context-driven buildings. Our architectural solutions are tailored to meet client objectives while responding to environmental, cultural, and functional requirements.

Our design process includes:
  • Concept development and feasibility studies
  • Detailed architectural drawings and documentation
  • 3D visualization and rendering
  • Sustainable and climate-responsive design strategies
Project Management

We provide professional project management services to ensure seamless execution from inception to completion. Our team oversees all phases of the project lifecycle, maintaining strict control over timelines, costs, and quality.

Key responsibilities include:
  • Project planning and coordination
  • Budgeting and cost control
  • Contractor and consultant management
  • Quality assurance and compliance monitoring
Model Making & Visualization

To enhance understanding and communication of design concepts, we produce high-quality physical and digital models. These tools enable clients and stakeholders to clearly visualize the final outcome before construction begins.

This includes:
  • Physical scale models
  • 3D digital models
  • Architectural renderings and presentations
Interior Design

We create interior spaces that are both functional and aesthetically refined. Our interior design solutions are tailored to reflect the client’s identity while enhancing user experience and spatial efficiency.

Scope of work:
  • Space planning and layout design
  • Material, color, and finish selection
  • Furniture and fixture specification
  • Lighting design concepts
Landscaping & External Works

We design outdoor environments that complement architectural structures and enhance overall spatial quality. Our landscaping solutions integrate nature, sustainability, and usability.

Services include:
  • Landscape design and planning
  • Outdoor space optimization
  • Integration of green and sustainable elements
SECTORS WE SERVE

  • Commercial Projects – Office spaces, retail outlets, and mixed-use developments
  • Institutional Projects – Educational, healthcare, and public facilities
  • Hospitality & Leisure – Hotels, resorts, and recreational spaces
