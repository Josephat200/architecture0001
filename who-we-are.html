Who We Are

Plankraft International is a dynamic, multi-disciplinary architectural firm based in Juja, Kenya, committed to delivering innovative and sustainable solutions within the built environment. As an emerging practice, the firm distinguishes itself through a thoughtful integration of creative design, technical expertise, and a deep understanding of client needs.

At Plankraft International, we view architecture as a powerful tool for shaping experiences, enhancing communities, and creating lasting value. Every project we undertake is approached with precision, professionalism, and a commitment to excellence.

Our Approach

Our approach is rooted in collaboration, innovation, and attention to detail. We work closely with clients, consultants, and stakeholders to ensure that each project reflects a clear vision while meeting functional, environmental, and economic requirements.

  • Design Excellence – delivering refined and contextually relevant solutions
  • Functionality – creating spaces that are efficient, practical, and user-centered
  • Sustainability – incorporating environmentally responsible and future-oriented design strategies
  • Integrity – upholding the highest standards of professionalism and accountability

Our Services

  • Architectural Design
    Development of innovative, functional, and aesthetically compelling building solutions.
  • Project Management
    Professional oversight of projects to ensure timely delivery, cost efficiency, and quality assurance.
  • Model Making
    Production of detailed physical and digital models to support visualization and design development.
  • Interior Design & Landscaping
    Creation of cohesive interior and exterior environments that enhance both form and function.

Our Vision

To be a leading architectural practice recognized for delivering innovative, sustainable, and impactful design solutions across the region and beyond.

Our Mission

To provide exceptional architectural and design services through a commitment to creativity, technical excellence, and client satisfaction.

Our Philosophy

Our work is anchored in the principle of “creating sustainably.” We are dedicated to designing spaces that respond to their environmental context while embracing modern architectural expression. By balancing aesthetics, functionality, and sustainability, we aim to deliver solutions that are both timeless and responsible.
